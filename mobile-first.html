Meaning and importance

Mobile first is a form of progressive enhancement and a web development and web design approach with focuses on prioritizing design and developmentfor mobile screen sized over design and development for desktop screen sizes, the reason behind the mobile first approach is the huge amount of mobile users that outnumber the desktop ones (63% to 37%) so it makes more sense to give the mobile users a smooth and complete experience
